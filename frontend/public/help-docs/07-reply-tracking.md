# How to Track and Reply to Inbound Emails in ColdAF

**Last Update:** July 2026

**Post ID:** 107

When your cold outreach starts working, replies come in fast. Managing those responses across multiple inboxes is a recipe for missed opportunities. ColdAF centralizes every reply in one place so you can **track email replies cold outreach** without switching tabs. A unified inbox means your team sees every response as it arrives, understands the sentiment at a glance, and replies quickly from the same platform where the campaign lives.

Learning how to **reply to cold email responses** inside ColdAF keeps your workflow tight. With threaded conversations, automatic sentiment tagging, and a clean split-pane layout, you can **cold email inbox management** directly inside the tool and turn replies into booked meetings faster than ever.

## You can track and reply to inbound emails in just a few steps. Here's how to do it:

1. Start with your main navigation, click on **Replies** in the left sidebar at the bottom and in the page that opens, you will see the split-pane reply manager.

2. In the **Replies** page, in the left pane at the center, you will see all reply conversations listed in chronological order. Each thread shows the lead name, subject line, and a sentiment badge.

3. Click on any conversation in the left pane to open the full thread in the right pane. You will see the complete message history with that lead, including your original email and every follow-up sent.

4. In the right pane, read the full message history and look at the **Sentiment** badge at the top of the thread. ColdAF tags each reply as **Positive**, **Neutral**, **Negative**, or **Out of Office** automatically so you can prioritize which leads to answer first.

5. Use the **Reply** box at the bottom of the right pane to compose your response. You can type your message, format it as needed, and add attachments if you want to include a deck, calendar link, or one-pager.

6. Click **Send** at the bottom of the reply box. Your response will be sent from the same sending address used in the campaign, so the thread stays consistent for the lead.

7. Mark conversations as read with the **Mark Read** button at the top of the thread, or use **Mark All Read** at the top of the left pane for bulk cleanup when you have cleared your queue.

That's it! Now you can manage every inbound reply from one screen and respond to leads without leaving ColdAF.

## Notes

- Replies are detected via **IMAP polling** every **5 minutes**, so new responses appear quickly without manual refreshing.
- **Sentiment analysis** tags each reply automatically based on the content and tone. This helps you spot hot leads and deprioritize uninterested contacts at a glance.
- The **threaded view** shows the full conversation history with that lead, including every campaign email and follow-up sent, so you always have context before you write back.
- You can reply directly from ColdAF—there is no need to open your external email client. The reply is sent from the same account that sent the campaign.
- The **Unread** count shows in the sidebar badge next to **Replies**, so you know immediately when new responses arrive without checking the page.

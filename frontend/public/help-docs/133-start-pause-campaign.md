# How to Start, Pause, or Resume a Campaign

**Last Update:** July 2026

**Post ID:** 133

Campaigns don't always run on a straight line. Sometimes you need to adjust timing, fix a template, or simply stop sends for a holiday. Knowing how to start cold email campaign coldaf workflows, pause them when needed, and resume without losing momentum is essential for any outreach team. In this guide, we'll cover the full lifecycle of campaign controls so you can pause campaign cold outreach activities safely and resume email campaign cold email flows exactly where you left off.

## You can start, pause, or resume a campaign in just a few steps. Here's how to do it:

1. On your **Dashboard** page, click on **Campaigns** in the left sidebar and in the **Campaigns** panel that opens, find the campaign you want to control.
2. In the **Campaigns** list, in the **Status** column at the right, click **Start** if the campaign is in **Draft** status.
3. To pause an active campaign, click **Pause** next to a campaign showing an **Active** badge.
4. To resume a paused campaign, click **Resume** next to a campaign showing a **Paused** badge.
5. In the confirmation dialog that appears, confirm your action.
6. Review the status change in the **Campaigns** list, then check the **Activity Feed** for a confirmation log entry.

That's it! Now you can control the exact rhythm of your outreach without rebuilding campaigns from scratch.

## Notes

- **Start** means emails begin sending according to the configured schedule and send windows.
- **Pause** stops new sends but keeps already-scheduled emails in the queue.
- **Resume** continues from where it left off, preserving the full sequence timeline.
- **Cancel** permanently stops a campaign and it cannot be resumed — use this only when you're sure.
- Active campaigns show a green badge for quick visual identification.
- All status changes are logged in **Audit Logs** for compliance and team visibility.

# How to Get Started with the ColdAF Dashboard

**Last Update:** July 2026

**Post ID:** 115

Your **cold email dashboard** is the first thing you see after logging in, and it is designed to give you a complete picture of your outreach performance at a glance. Whether you are checking morning send volume or reviewing weekly campaign health, the **cold email analytics overview** surfaces the metrics that matter most. Understanding how to read and interact with the Dashboard helps you move faster from observation to action. This article introduces the **cold outreach campaign dashboard** layout, explains each KPI card and chart, and shows you how to filter data by date range so you always know exactly how your campaigns are performing.

## You can get started with the Dashboard in just a few steps. Here's how to do it:

Log in to ColdAF at coldaf.ataflexsolutions.com. You'll land on the **Dashboard** automatically after authentication.

At the top of the page, use the **Date Range** selector to choose **7 Days**, **30 Days**, or **90 Days**. This filter applies to all charts and KPIs simultaneously.

Review the **KPI cards** in the top row. These show Total Sent, Delivered, Opened, Replied, Clicked, Bounced, Unsubscribed, and Complaints. Each card has a gradient icon container that makes the metric type easy to spot quickly.

Scroll down to the **Email Volume** chart to see sends over time. This AreaChart shows your daily sending pattern and helps you spot volume spikes or drops.

Check the **Campaign Status** pie chart to see how many campaigns are Active, Paused, Completed, or Cancelled. This gives you a quick sense of pipeline health.

Review the **Recent Activity** feed on the right side for the latest sends, opens, and replies. This feed shows the last 50 events in real time.

Click any KPI card to drill down to the relevant page. For example, clicking **Replied** takes you to the **Replies** page where you can respond to inbound messages.

Click any chart element to filter the underlying data. For example, clicking the **Paused** slice in the pie chart will filter the campaign list to show only paused campaigns.

Toggle dark mode if you prefer by clicking the dark mode toggle in the header.

That's it! Now you can use the Dashboard as your daily command center for all outreach activity.

## Notes

- The Dashboard is your command center for all cold outreach activity and should be your first stop every day.
- All data is real-time and reflects your organization's campaigns only, so there is no cross-tenant leakage if you **manage multiple brands cold outreach**.
- The date range applies to all charts and KPIs simultaneously, so the numbers are always aligned.
- Click any chart element to filter the underlying data without leaving the page.
- The activity feed shows the last 50 events and refreshes automatically as new activity arrives.
- Dark mode is available via the toggle in the header and persists across sessions.
- The glassmorphism design keeps the interface clean while highlighting the metrics that need attention.

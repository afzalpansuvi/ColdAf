# How to View AI Agent Logs in ColdAF

**Last Update:** July 2026

**Post ID:** 151

Every decision your AI agents make should be transparent. If you need to view ai agent results cold outreach history, the agent logs give you a complete audit trail of every check. Reviewing ai agent logs coldaf records helps you understand why an agent recommended a pause, a limit change, or a prompt update. Keeping an ai activity history cold email log is also important for compliance and team training, since you can see exactly what the AI evaluated and when. Logs turn black-box automation into something you can trust and refine over time.

The log viewer is built into the **AI Agent** page, so you do not need to export data to another system. You can filter by date, drill into individual runs, and review token usage for each check. This makes it easy to spot patterns, such as an agent repeatedly flagging the same SMTP account, or token usage spiking during busy campaign periods. Visibility into logs builds confidence in the AI system.

## You can view AI agent logs in just a few steps. Here's how to do it:

1. Go to **AI Agent** from the main navigation menu.
2. Select an agent from the list, such as the CEO or Cold Email Specialist.
3. Click the **View Logs** tab inside the agent detail panel.
4. Review the log entries, each showing a timestamp, status, and a brief summary of the check.
5. Click any log entry to open the full report details, including the AI's reasoning and findings.
6. Check the tokens used for that specific run to understand the cost of the check.
7. Review the actions taken or proposed during that run.
8. Use the date range filter to narrow down logs to a specific week or month.
9. Click **Export** if you need to download the logs for external reporting or archiving.

That's it! Now you have a complete history of AI agent activity for your organization.

## Notes

- Logs show what the AI checked, what it found, and what actions it proposed or executed.
- Successful runs show a green status indicator, while failed runs show red with detailed error information.
- Each log entry includes the full AI reasoning, so you can audit why a recommendation was made.
- Token usage is tracked per log entry and rolls up into the **AI Usage** dashboard.
- Logs are retained for 90 days; older entries are automatically purged.
- Use logs to audit AI decisions and train your team on what the agents are watching.
- You can filter by status to quickly find failed checks that need attention.
- Exported logs are provided in CSV format for easy analysis in spreadsheet tools.

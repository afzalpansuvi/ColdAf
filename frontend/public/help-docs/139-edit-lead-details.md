# How to Edit Lead Details in ColdAF

**Last Update:** July 2026

**Post ID:** 139

Lead data changes all the time. People switch roles, companies rebrand, and contact details get updated. Being able to **edit lead coldaf** records quickly keeps your outreach accurate and professional. This article explains how to **update lead information cold email** profiles so your campaigns stay relevant. Whether you are correcting a typo or refreshing a job title, learning how to **modify cold outreach contact** details will help you maintain a high-quality database and improve your reply rates over time. Outdated data leads to poor personalization and higher unsubscribe rates, so regular updates are a best practice for any serious outreach team.

## You can edit lead details in just a few steps. Here's how to do it:

1. Go to **Leads** from the main navigation.
2. Find the lead you want to update in the list. You can use the search bar or filters to narrow results.
3. Click the lead name or the **Edit** button on the lead row.
4. Update any fields as needed: name, email, company, title, industry, phone, or notes.
5. Change the lead status if necessary, for example, from **New** to **Qualified** or **Unsubscribed**.
6. Click **Save Changes** to apply the updates.
7. Review the updated lead in the detail view to confirm everything is correct.

That's it! Now you can **edit lead coldaf** records and keep your contact data accurate.

## Notes

- Email changes trigger automatic re-verification to ensure the new address is valid and deliverable.
- Status changes affect campaign eligibility. For example, **Unsubscribed** leads are excluded from active sequences automatically.
- Company and title changes improve future AI personalization because templates pull from these fields.
- Edit history is tracked in **Audit Logs** so you can see who changed what and when.
- You can also bulk edit leads from the list view by selecting multiple leads with checkboxes and using the bulk actions menu.
- Keeping lead details current reduces bounce rates and improves the relevance of your messaging.
- You can add custom fields to leads if your organization has configured additional data points in **Settings**.

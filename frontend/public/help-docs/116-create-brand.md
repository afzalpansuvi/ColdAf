# How to Create a Brand in ColdAF

**Last Update:** July 2026

**Post ID:** 116

If you run an agency or manage cold outreach for multiple clients, you need a way to keep each identity separate. Learning how to create brand cold email setups inside a single platform saves you from juggling multiple logins and spreadsheets. With ColdAF, you can spin up a dedicated brand for every client or business unit, complete with its own domain, logo, timezone, and sending rules. This is the foundation of any multi brand cold outreach strategy, and it takes just a few minutes to get right.

A well-organized brand setup also protects your sender reputation. When each client has their own domain, signature, and AI prompt, your emails look personal and professional rather than mass-blasted. If you have been looking for a straightforward coldaf brand setup guide, this article walks you through every field and setting.

## You can create a brand in just a few steps. Here's how to do it:

1. On your **Dashboard** page, click on **Brands** in the left sidebar and in the panel that opens, click **Create Brand**.
2. In **Brands**, in the form at the center, enter the **Brand Name** and **Domain** for this client or business.
3. Click the **Logo** upload area and select an image file from your computer. Use a PNG or JPG under 2 MB for best results.
4. In the **Timezone** dropdown at the top-right of the form, choose the timezone where this brand operates. This affects send scheduling and reporting.
5. In the **Daily Send Limit** field, enter the maximum number of emails this brand should send per day across all assigned accounts.
6. Click the **AI Prompt** tab and enter a custom prompt that defines the brand voice and tone for AI-generated emails.
7. Click the **Email Signatures** tab and create or assign a signature that will be automatically appended to campaign emails.
8. In the **SMTP Accounts** section at the bottom, click **Assign Account** and select the sending accounts that belong to this brand.
9. Review all settings, then click **Save** at the top-right of the page.

That's it! Now you can manage this brand as a fully isolated workspace with its own identity, sending limits, and AI voice.

## Notes

- Each brand is isolated. Contacts, campaigns, analytics, and templates are completely separate from other brands, which makes ColdAF perfect for agencies managing multiple clients.
- You can switch between brands instantly using the brand selector in the page header, so you never have to log out and back in.
- The daily send limit you set at the brand level is enforced across all SMTP accounts assigned to that brand. If you need higher volume, add more accounts or raise the limit.
- We recommend creating one brand per domain to keep authentication, tracking, and reputation cleanly separated.

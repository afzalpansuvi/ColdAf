# How to Use AI to Generate Cold Emails in ColdAF

**Last Update:** July 2026

**Post ID:** 110

Writing personalized cold emails at scale is one of the hardest parts of outbound. Every lead deserves a message that feels personal, but doing that manually for many contacts is not realistic. ColdAF gives you an **AI cold email generator** built directly into the template editor, so you can draft compelling, personalized emails in seconds and have full control to edit and approve before sending.

Whether you need an **AI generate cold emails** workflow for a single campaign or a repeatable system across every brand, ColdAF connects to the best models in the industry. With support for **Claude**, **GPT**, and **Gemini**, you can choose the provider that fits your style. The platform injects real lead context so every email feels researched rather than mass-produced. If you are looking for an **AI email writing tool cold outreach** solution that balances speed with quality, ColdAF is built for that.

## You can use AI to generate cold emails in just a few steps. Here's how to do it:

1. Start with your main navigation, click on **Templates** in the left sidebar at the center and in the **Templates** page that opens, click **Create Template**.

2. In the template creation form, in the **Template Name** field at the top, enter a name for your template (for example, "SaaS Founder Outreach v1").

3. In the **Body** section at the center, click the **AI Generate** button. A panel will open where you can choose your AI provider and enter your prompt.

4. In the AI panel, select your AI provider from the dropdown: **Claude**, **GPT**, or **Gemini**. Pick the one that matches your brand voice.

5. In the **Prompt** field, enter a description of the email you want. For example, "Write a friendly cold email to a SaaS founder about our outbound automation tool."

6. Add lead context variables to the template body. Use variables like **{{firstName}}**, **{{company}}**, and **{{role}}** so ColdAF can personalize the email for each lead when the campaign runs.

7. Click **Generate** in the AI panel. The selected AI will write a draft email based on your prompt and the variables you included.

8. Review the AI-generated draft in the body editor. Edit the message if needed to match your exact tone.

9. Click **Save Template** at the top right. When you create a campaign, select this template and ColdAF will automatically personalize each email with the lead data from your list.

That's it! Now you can draft high-quality, personalized cold emails in seconds and scale them across any campaign.

## Notes

- Each **brand** can have its own AI prompt that defines tone and style. This means your serious corporate brand and casual startup brand can each have an AI personality.
- The AI uses **lead context**—including company, role, and industry—to tailor the message. The more variables you include, the more personalized the output.
- **Token usage** is tracked per generation and shown in the **AI Usage** dashboard. You can monitor costs across your team.
- If you do not like the first draft, click **Generate** again to get a fresh version without losing your prompt.
- **AI-generated templates** can still include **spintax** for further variation. Combine AI-generated copy with spintax syntax to create even more unique messages at scale.

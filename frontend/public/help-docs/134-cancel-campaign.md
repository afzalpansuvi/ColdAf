# How to Cancel a Campaign in ColdAF

**Last Update:** July 2026

**Post ID:** 134

Sometimes a campaign needs to be shut down permanently — maybe the offer changed, the list was wrong, or the timing shifted. Knowing how to cancel campaign coldaf workflows properly ensures that no more emails slip out and your brand stays protected. Canceling is different from pausing because it cannot be undone. In this guide, we'll show you how to stop cold email campaign coldaf sends completely and what happens to your leads and data when you do.

## You can cancel a campaign in just a few steps. Here's how to do it:

1. On your **Dashboard** page, click on **Campaigns** in the left sidebar and in the **Campaigns** panel that opens, find the campaign you want to end.
2. In the **Campaigns** list, in the **Actions** column at the far right, click **Cancel**.
3. In the confirmation dialog that appears, confirm the cancellation.
4. Review that all scheduled emails are stopped and the campaign status changes to **Cancelled**.
5. Check the **Leads** tab to confirm leads are unassigned from this campaign.

That's it! Now the campaign is permanently stopped and no further emails will be sent.

## Notes

- **Cancel** is permanent — the campaign cannot be resumed after cancellation.
- All queued emails are cleared immediately upon cancellation.
- Sent emails remain in history and are still visible in reports and analytics.
- Lead assignment is removed, so those leads can be reassigned to a new campaign later.
- Use **Pause** if you plan to resume the campaign later instead of canceling.
- Cancelled campaigns stay in the list for reporting and reference purposes.
- If you are on metered billing, there are no refunds for cancelled campaigns.

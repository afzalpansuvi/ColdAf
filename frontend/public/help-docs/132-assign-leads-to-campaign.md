# How to Assign Leads to a Campaign

**Last Update:** July 2026

**Post ID:** 132

A campaign is only as good as the leads it reaches. Before any emails go out, you need to assign the right prospects to the right campaign. Learning how to assign leads campaign coldaf workflows is critical for keeping your outreach organized and compliant. Whether you're importing fresh contacts or pulling from an existing pool, the campaign lead assignment cold outreach process in ColdAF is designed to be fast and flexible. In this guide, we'll show you how to add leads to cold email campaign setups so you can start targeting with precision.

## You can assign leads to a campaign in just a few steps. Here's how to do it:

1. On your **Dashboard** page, click on **Campaigns** in the left sidebar and in the **Campaigns** panel that opens, select the campaign you want to populate.
2. In the **Campaign Details** page, in the **Leads** tab at the top, click **Assign Leads**.
3. In the **Assign Leads** dialog, choose from existing leads and filter by status, source, or score to narrow the list.
4. Select individual leads with checkboxes, or use the **Select All** toggle, then click **Assign Selected**.
5. Alternatively, in the **Upload** section at the bottom, upload a CSV directly to the campaign and map the required fields.
6. Review the assigned lead count in the **Summary** bar, then click **Save** to confirm.

That's it! Now your leads are tied to the campaign and will begin receiving emails according to your sequence schedule.

## Notes

- Only leads with verified email addresses can be assigned to a campaign.
- Leads already in another active campaign may be excluded to avoid double-sending.
- You can assign leads in batches rather than all at once for better control.
- Unassigned leads are shown separately and can be assigned later.
- Assigned leads can be removed before the campaign starts, but not after the first email is sent.

# How to Use Spintax in Your Email Templates in ColdAF

**Last Update:** July 2026

**Post ID:** 145

Email providers and spam filters can flag identical messages sent in bulk. Spintax solves this by introducing random variation into your templates so every email looks slightly different. Learning how to use **spintax cold email template** syntax helps you avoid duplicate detection and keep your outreach feeling natural. This guide explains **spintax syntax coldaf** and shows you how to add **email variation cold outreach template** elements that keep your campaigns fresh without sacrificing your core message. When used correctly, spintax can improve your inbox placement rates and make your messaging feel more human at scale.

## You can use spintax in your email templates in just a few steps. Here's how to do it:

1. Go to **Templates** from the main navigation.
2. Create a new template or click **Edit** on an existing one.
3. In the subject line or body, write spintax syntax where you want variation. For example: **{{Hi|Hello|Hey}}** {{firstName}}.
4. Add multiple variations for key phrases. For example: **{{quick question|quick idea|quick thought}}** about {{company}}.
5. Include spintax for greetings, CTAs, opening lines, or subject lines to maximize variation across sends.
6. Click **Preview** to see a random variation generated from your spintax.
7. Test with multiple previews to see different combinations and ensure all outputs read naturally.
8. Click **Save** to store the template with spintax included.

That's it! Now you can use **spintax syntax coldaf** and send unique variations of every message automatically.

## Notes

- Spintax means spin syntax. It creates random variation by selecting one option from each set.
- The format is **{{option1|option2|option3}}**. ColdAF randomly picks one option for each send.
- Use spintax for greetings, calls to action, opening lines, and subject lines.
- It is great for avoiding duplicate detection by email providers and keeping deliverability high.
- Combine spintax with AI generation for maximum variation across large campaigns.
- Do not overdo it. Every variation should still be readable and professional.
- The preview shows one random combination each time. Click preview multiple times to test different outcomes.
- Spintax works alongside merge variables like **{{firstName}}**, so you can combine personalization with variation in the same template.

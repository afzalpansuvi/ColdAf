# How to Create a Cold Email Campaign in ColdAF

**Last Update:** July 2026

**Post ID:** 102

Launching a well-structured campaign is how you turn cold leads into warm conversations. ColdAF makes it easy to **create cold email campaign** workflows that scale across multiple brands, follow up automatically, and respect your leads' time zones. Whether you're running your first **cold outreach campaign setup** or managing dozens of sequences, this guide shows you exactly how to **launch cold email campaign** from start to finish. A good campaign includes the right leads, the right message, and the right timing — ColdAF handles all three in one place.

## You can create a campaign in just a few steps. Here's how to do it:

1. On your **Dashboard** page, click on **Campaigns** in the left sidebar, and in the page that opens, click **Create Campaign** at the top right.

2. In the **Create Campaign** modal, enter a **Campaign Name** and optionally a **Description**. Then select one or more **Brands** from the dropdown. ColdAF will rotate sending across these brands automatically.

3. In the **Leads** section, assign leads by selecting an existing list from the dropdown, or click **Upload CSV** to import a new file directly. You can filter leads by status before assigning them.

4. In the **Email Template** section, choose an existing template from **Templates**, or click **Generate with AI** to have ColdAF create a personalized email using the AI Chat or AI Agent features.

5. In the **Sequence Builder** section, configure your follow-up steps. Click **Add Step** to create a follow-up email, set the delay (e.g., 3 days), and choose whether to send on the same thread or start a new one. The sequence will automatically stop if a lead replies or unsubscribes.

6. In the **Send Window** section, define the hours and days when emails should be sent. ColdAF will automatically respect each lead's timezone based on their location data.

7. Click **Start Campaign** to launch. You can pause or resume the campaign at any time from the **Campaigns** list by clicking **Pause** or **Resume** next to the campaign name.

That's it! Now your campaign is live and ColdAF will begin sending emails according to your schedule.

## Notes

- You can assign **multiple brands** to a single campaign. ColdAF rotates sends across them, which helps maintain deliverability and protects any single account from hitting rate limits.
- Leads can be filtered by status (e.g., "New", "Contacted", "Replied") before assignment. Only leads that match your filter will enter the campaign.
- The **Sequence Builder** stops sending follow-ups automatically if a lead replies or clicks unsubscribe. You can override this behavior in **Settings** if needed.
- **Send windows** are defined in your local time, but ColdAF converts them to each lead's timezone automatically using the location data on their profile.
- For A/B testing, create multiple template variants in the **Email Template** step. See [How to Set Up A/B Testing for Cold Emails in ColdAF] for the full guide.

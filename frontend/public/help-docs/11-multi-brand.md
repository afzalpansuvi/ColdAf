# How to Manage Multiple Brands in ColdAF

**Last Update:** July 2026

**Post ID:** 111

Managing multiple brands or clients from a single platform is one of the biggest challenges for agencies and sales teams running **multi brand cold email management** workflows. ColdAF is built specifically for this scenario, giving you a true multi-tenant environment where each brand operates in complete isolation. Whether you run a cold email agency or handle outreach for several in-house divisions, learning how to **manage multiple brands cold outreach** in one tool saves hours of context switching and keeps every client's data separated. This article walks you through setting up each brand with its own identity, send limits, and AI voice so your team can scale without stepping on anyone's reputation.

## You can set up multiple brands in just a few steps. Here's how to do it:

On your **Dashboard** page, click on **Brands** in the left navigation menu and in the panel that opens, click **Create Brand**.

In the **Brands** page, in the **Create Brand** form at the top, click on **Upload Logo** to add the brand's visual identity.

Enter the brand name and domain in the corresponding fields, then select the timezone from the dropdown so scheduling and reporting reflect the correct local time.

Set the daily send limit in the **Send Limits** section to protect this brand's sender reputation and avoid triggering provider rate limits.

Configure the **AI Prompt** that defines this brand's tone, style, and messaging personality so every AI-generated email sounds consistent with the client's voice.

Add the **Email Signatures** for this brand in the signature editor, including name, title, and any legal disclaimers.

In the **SMTP Accounts** section, assign or create SMTP accounts that are specific to this brand's domain and sending needs.

Add a **Custom Tracking Domain** under the tracking domain settings so links in this brand's emails carry its own domain instead of a generic one.

Click **Save Brand** to finalize the configuration. Repeat this process for each client brand your team manages.

When creating campaigns, select the appropriate brand from the **Brand** dropdown in the campaign setup form so all sends, replies, and analytics are attributed to the correct tenant.

That's it! Now you can manage multiple isolated brands from a single ColdAF workspace.

## Notes

- Each brand is completely isolated — leads, campaigns, and analytics are separated, so client data never mixes.
- This design is perfect for agencies managing multiple clients under one roof.
- Brand-level AI prompts ensure each client's voice is consistent across every touchpoint.
- Send limits protect each brand's reputation independently; one brand hitting a ceiling won't affect others.
- You can switch between brands quickly using the brand selector in the header without logging out.
- A **cold email agency tool** needs this separation to bill clients accurately and keep permissions scoped.

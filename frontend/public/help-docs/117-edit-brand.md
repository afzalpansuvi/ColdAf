# How to Edit or Update a Brand in ColdAF

**Last Update:** July 2026

**Post ID:** 117

Business details change. A client might rebrand, switch domains, or adjust their daily sending volume. Knowing how to edit brand coldaf settings keeps your outreach accurate and prevents emails from going out with outdated signatures or wrong domains. Whether you need to tweak a logo, shift a timezone, or refine an AI voice, updating a brand is faster than building one from scratch.

If you are running a busy agency, you will regularly need to update brand settings cold email clients rely on. This article shows you exactly where to click and what to watch for so your changes take effect safely without disrupting live campaigns.

## You can edit or update a brand in just a few steps. Here's how to do it:

1. On your **Dashboard** page, click on **Brands** in the left sidebar and in the panel that opens, locate the brand you want to update in the list.
2. In **Brands**, in the row for the target brand at the right side, click on **Edit**.
3. In the form that appears, update the **Brand Name**, **Domain**, or **Logo** as needed. Click the logo upload area to replace the existing image.
4. In the **Timezone** dropdown at the top-right of the form, select a new timezone if the brand's operating region has changed.
5. In the **Daily Send Limit** field, adjust the number to reflect the new volume cap for this brand.
6. Click the **AI Prompt** tab and modify the description, tone, or example emails to match any updated brand voice.
7. Click the **Email Signatures** tab and update the signature text, title, or logo if the brand's contact details have changed.
8. Review all changes, then click **Save** at the top-right of the page.

That's it! Now your brand reflects the latest information and will use the updated settings for all future outreach.

## Notes

- Changes to the AI prompt only affect future campaigns. Emails that have already been generated or queued will keep the previous prompt.
- Domain changes require re-verification. If you update the domain field, make sure DNS records and tracking domains are reconfigured before sending resumes.
- Send limit changes take effect immediately. If you lower the limit below today's already-sent count, ColdAF will pause further sends until the next reset window.
- You cannot change the brand's creation date or historical analytics, but all new activity will flow under the updated configuration.

# How to Chat with the AI CEO Agent in ColdAF

**Last Update:** July 2026

**Post ID:** 149

Sometimes you need a quick answer about your outreach performance without digging through dozens of charts. If you are looking for an ai chat coldaf experience that actually understands your campaigns, the AI CEO Agent is designed to do exactly that. Using an ai assistant cold email tool like this means you can ask natural questions and get actionable insights in seconds. The ceo agent cold outreach feature goes beyond simple lookup; it analyzes your data, identifies trends, and even proposes specific actions you can take to improve results. This is ideal for busy founders and managers who need to make decisions fast without opening every analytics tab.

The chat interface is built into ColdAF so you do not need external tools. You can ask strategic questions like which campaign is underperforming, what your best send time is, or whether you should pause a sequence. The agent has access to your full campaign data, analytics, and settings, so its answers are grounded in real numbers rather than generic advice. Over time, the agent learns your preferences and gives sharper recommendations.

## You can chat with the AI CEO Agent in just a few steps. Here's how to do it:

1. Go to **AI Chat** from the main navigation menu.
2. Click inside the chat input field at the bottom of the panel.
3. Type your question. For example: "Which campaign has the lowest open rate this week?" or "Pause my underperforming campaigns."
4. Press **Enter** to send the message to the AI CEO Agent.
5. Wait a few seconds while the agent analyzes your data and formulates a response.
6. Review the AI's response, which includes analysis and a proposed action when applicable.
7. If the agent proposes an action, click **Confirm** if you want it to execute the change, or click **Dismiss** if you only wanted advice.
8. Scroll up to review your conversation history, which is saved automatically for future reference.

That's it! Now you can get strategic insights and execute changes through natural conversation.

## Notes

- The CEO Agent has access to all campaign data, analytics, and settings within your organization.
- It can propose actions like pausing campaigns, adjusting send limits, or updating AI prompts.
- You must confirm all proposed actions before they execute; the AI never makes changes without your approval.
- Chat history is stored per user, so only you can see your own conversations.
- The AI learns from your organization data over time, so answers become more relevant the longer you use it.
- Use it for strategic questions and recommendations, not just for raw data lookup.
- Complex questions may take a few seconds to process depending on the size of your data.
- You can reference specific campaigns by name to get faster, more precise answers.

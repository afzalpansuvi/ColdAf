## You can generate a cold email with AI in just a few steps. Here's how to do it:

1. Go to **Templates** from the left sidebar navigation.
2. Click **Create Template** to open the template editor.
3. Give your template a clear name so you can find it later in the list.
4. Fill in the subject line field with a working title, or leave it blank if you plan to generate the subject later.
5. Scroll to the **Body** section of the editor where you write the email content.
6. Click **AI Generate** inside the body editor to open the AI assistant panel.
7. Select your AI provider from the list: **Claude** (Anthropic), **GPT** (OpenAI), or **Gemini** (Google).
8. Enter a detailed prompt in the input field. For example: "Write a friendly cold email to a SaaS founder about our outbound tool, mentioning how we helped a similar company increase reply rates by 40%."
9. Add personalization variables like **{{firstName}}** and **{{company}}** where you want them to appear in the draft.
10. Click **Generate** and wait a few seconds for the AI to produce a draft based on your prompt and brand context.
11. Review the generated draft carefully. Edit the text if the tone, length, or details need tweaking.
12. Click **Save Template** to store it for use in any campaign.

That's it! Now you can use your AI-generated template inside any campaign and send personalized emails at scale.

## Notes

- AI uses your brand prompt plus your custom prompt for context, so the more detail you provide in your brand settings, the better the output.
- Each generation tracks tokens automatically in the **AI Usage** dashboard under the main menu.
- If you do not like the draft, click **Generate** again for a fresh version without losing your original prompt.
- AI-generated templates can include spintax to create natural language variations across sends.
- Best results come from detailed prompts that include specific value propositions, audience details, and desired tone.
- Token costs vary depending on the model you choose; Claude Sonnet costs more than Gemini Flash.
- You can always edit the generated text manually before saving to add company-specific details the AI may not know.
- Generated drafts maintain the formatting and line breaks you specify in the prompt, so be explicit about structure if it matters.

# How to Create a Lead Manually in ColdAF

**Last Update:** July 2026

**Post ID:** 138

Not every lead comes from a bulk list. Sometimes you meet a prospect at a conference, receive a referral, or find a single contact worth adding directly. Knowing how to **create lead manually coldaf** is essential for those one-off situations. This guide covers the **manual lead entry cold outreach** process so you can add a single contact quickly and keep your pipeline complete. Adding leads one by one also helps you **add single lead cold email** records with full context, which improves personalization and targeting later. Manual entry is also useful for testing campaigns before launching them to a larger audience, since you can send a draft to yourself or a teammate first.

## You can create a lead manually in just a few steps. Here's how to do it:

1. Go to **Leads** from the main navigation menu.
2. Click **Create Lead** at the top of the leads list.
3. Fill in the **First Name** field.
4. Fill in the **Last Name** field.
5. Enter the **Email** address. This field is required and will be validated automatically.
6. Fill in optional fields for better context: **Company**, **Title**, **Industry**, **Phone**, **Website**, and **LinkedIn URL**.
7. Select a **Source** from the dropdown, such as Manual, Web, Referral, or another option.
8. If you want to assign the lead to a campaign immediately, select the campaign in the assignment field.
9. Click **Save Lead** to store the record.

That's it! Now you can **create lead manually coldaf** and enrich your lead database with complete contact details.

## Notes

- **Email** is required and validated automatically. Invalid emails will be rejected.
- **Company** and **Title** improve AI personalization and make merge fields more effective in templates.
- **Source** helps you track where the lead came from and measure channel performance over time.
- Leads can be assigned to a campaign immediately, or you can leave them unassigned and batch them later.
- Manual leads start with a status of **New** until a campaign sends an email to them.
- You can add additional notes in the lead detail page after saving.

# How to Schedule White-Label Reports in ColdAF

**Last Update:** July 2026

**Post ID:** 113

Agencies and sales teams need to prove ROI to clients, but building manual reports every week is a time sink. ColdAF solves this with **white label cold email reports** that strip out platform branding and replace it with your own. Whether you need **automated email analytics reports** sent to stakeholders every Monday or monthly **client reporting cold outreach** summaries delivered as PDFs, the scheduling engine handles the heavy lifting. This article explains how to configure, brand, and automate reports so your clients see professional deliverables without you touching a spreadsheet.

## You can schedule white-label reports in just a few steps. Here's how to do it:

On your **Dashboard** page, click on **Reports** in the left navigation menu and in the panel that opens, click **Schedule Report**.

In the **Reports** page, in the **Schedule Report** form at the top, enter a name for the report so you can identify it later in the list.

Select the report type from the dropdown: choose **Campaign Performance** for detailed send metrics, **Brand Overview** for multi-brand summaries, **Deliverability Score** for reputation health, or **Custom** if you want to build your own mix of metrics.

Select the brand(s) and date range the report should cover. If you manage multiple clients, you can scope each report to a single brand or aggregate across your organization.

Choose the format: click **PDF** for polished charts and tables, or click **CSV** for raw data you can import into other tools.

Upload your agency logo in the **White-Label Settings** section and set the brand colors so the report matches your visual identity.

Set the schedule by clicking **Daily**, **Weekly**, or **Monthly** depending on how often your clients expect updates.

Add recipient email addresses in the **Recipients** field. These are usually your clients or internal stakeholders who need the data.

Click **Preview** to see sample data and confirm the layout, branding, and metrics look correct before activating.

Click **Save & Activate** to start the automated delivery schedule.

After the first cycle, view the **Delivery Logs** tab to confirm emails were sent, check for failures, and see whether any bounced.

That's it! Now your clients will receive automated, branded reports on schedule.

## Notes

- White-label reports hide all ColdAF branding and show your agency logo and colors instead, so recipients never see the underlying platform.
- PDF reports include charts and tables formatted for presentation; CSV reports include raw data for further analysis in Excel or Google Sheets.
- Delivery logs show sent, failed, and bounced statuses, so you can troubleshoot recipient addresses quickly.
- You can send a report manually at any time with the **Send Now** button without affecting the regular schedule.
- Reports can be scheduled per brand, so each client receives only their own data and never sees another client's metrics.
- This feature is especially useful when you **manage multiple brands cold outreach** and need to deliver separate reports for each client automatically.

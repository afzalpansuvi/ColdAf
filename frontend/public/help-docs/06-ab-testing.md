# How to Set Up A/B Testing for Cold Emails in ColdAF

**Last Update:** July 2026

**Post ID:** 106

Running cold outreach without testing is like flying blind. A/B testing helps you discover what works—whether it is a subject line that boosts opens, a body angle that drives replies, or a send time that lands at the perfect moment. With **cold email ab testing** built directly into ColdAF, you can run statistically valid split tests without relying on guesswork. Learning how to **split test cold emails** inside ColdAF means every campaign gets smarter as it runs, so your team spends less time wondering and more time booking meetings.

If you want to **ab test subject lines cold outreach** and roll the winner out automatically, ColdAF makes it simple. The platform handles traffic allocation and winner selection so you can focus on messaging while the data does the talking.

## You can set up A/B testing in just a few steps. Here's how to do it:

1. Start with your **Campaigns** page, click on the campaign you want to test at the center of the list and in the panel that opens, click **A/B Testing**.

2. In the campaign detail page, in the **A/B Testing** tab at the top, click on **Create A/B Test**.

3. In the test creation form, in the **Name** field at the top, enter a name for your test. Then, in the **Test Type** dropdown at the center, choose what you want to test: **Subject Line**, **Email Body**, or **Send Time**.

4. In the **Variant A** section, create your control version. In the **Variant B** section, create your challenger with the changed element. For example, if testing subject lines, write one subject in Variant A and a different subject in Variant B.

5. In the **Traffic Allocation** section at the bottom, set the split for each variant (for example, **50%** for Variant A and **50%** for Variant B). Then set the **Minimum Sample Size** and **Test Duration** so ColdAF knows when it has enough data to evaluate results.

6. If you want the platform to pick the winner automatically, enable **Auto-Optimize** in the settings panel. ColdAF will declare the winner once the results reach statistical significance.

7. Click **Start Test** at the bottom. The test will begin sending and ColdAF will track opens, replies, and clicks for each variant.

8. View live results in the **A/B Test** panel. You will see open rates, reply rates, and the calculated **Statistical Significance** for each variant so you know when the data is strong enough to act on.

That's it! Now you can run data-backed experiments inside any campaign and automatically roll out the winning version to your entire audience.

## Notes

- **Auto-Optimize** requires at least **100 sends per variant** before it can declare a winner. This ensures the decision is based on real data, not noise.
- Statistical significance is calculated automatically and shown in the results panel. Do not stop a test early just because one variant is slightly ahead.
- You can declare a winner manually at any time if you prefer to make the call yourself.
- Once a winner is selected, the winning variant can be rolled out to **100%** of the remaining traffic with one click.

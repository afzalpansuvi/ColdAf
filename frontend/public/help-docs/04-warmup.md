# How to Warm Up Your Email Address in ColdAF

**Last Update:** July 2026

**Post ID:** 104

Sending hundreds of emails from a brand-new account is a fast track to the spam folder. That's why **email warmup cold outreach** is essential before you launch real campaigns. ColdAF's built-in warmup system gradually builds your sender reputation by simulating real email traffic — opens, replies, and back-and-forth conversations — all managed automatically. This guide walks you through the best way to **warm up email account** and explains why a proper **cold email warmup guide** can make the difference between landing in the inbox and disappearing into spam.

## You can warm up your email address in just a few steps. Here's how to do it:

1. On your **Dashboard** page, click on **Warmup** in the left sidebar, and in the page that opens, click **Create Warmup Campaign** at the top right.

2. In the **Create Warmup Campaign** modal, select an existing **Warmup Pool** from the dropdown, or click **Create New Pool** to add a group of email accounts. A pool is a collection of accounts that will exchange warmup emails with each other.

3. In the **Ramp Strategy** section, choose either **Linear** or **Exponential**. Linear increases your daily volume by a fixed number of emails each day. Exponential increases it by a percentage, which grows faster over time.

4. In the **Daily Volume** section, set your **Starting Daily Volume** (e.g., 5 emails) and your **Target Daily Volume** (e.g., 50 emails). Also set the **Duration** in weeks (recommended: 2 to 4 weeks for best results).

5. In the **Engagement Simulation** section, enable **Opens** and **Replies** to make the warmup activity look natural to ISPs. ColdAF will simulate realistic open rates and reply threads between accounts in your pool. You can also enable **AI-generated warmup content** so emails vary in topic and length.

6. Click **Start** to begin the warmup. Your campaign will appear on the **Warmup** dashboard with live stats including emails sent, opens, replies, and ramp progress.

That's it! Now your email address is building reputation while you prepare your real campaigns.

## Notes

- **Warmup protects sender reputation** before you ever send a real campaign. Skipping warmup is one of the most common reasons new accounts get blacklisted or throttled by ISPs.
- Start with **5 to 10 emails per day** and ramp up over **2 to 4 weeks**. Rushing this process reduces its effectiveness.
- A **Linear ramp** increases by a fixed number of emails per day (e.g., +5 each day). An **Exponential ramp** increases by a percentage (e.g., +20% each day), which means it grows faster in later weeks. Choose Linear for steady, predictable growth and Exponential for faster scaling.
- You can keep **warmup running alongside real campaigns**. The two systems are independent. ColdAF will never send warmup emails to your real leads.
- **Engagement simulation** is what makes warmup effective. ISPs look at open rates, reply rates, and time-on-page. ColdAF mimics all of these so your account looks like a real, active mailbox.
- Monitor your warmup stats on the **Warmup** dashboard. If you see a sudden drop in engagement, check your SMTP account health in the **SMTP Accounts** page.

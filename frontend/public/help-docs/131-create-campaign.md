# How to Create a Cold Email Campaign in ColdAF

**Last Update:** July 2026

**Post ID:** 131

Launching a campaign is the core of what ColdAF was built for. Whether you're prospecting for a new agency client or scaling a multi-brand outreach program, knowing how to create cold email campaign coldaf workflows efficiently will get you to inbox faster. A well-structured cold outreach campaign setup also sets the stage for better deliverability, cleaner sequences, and clearer analytics. In this guide, you'll learn how to launch cold email campaign cold email projects from scratch using ColdAF's built-in campaign builder.

## You can create a cold email campaign in just a few steps. Here's how to do it:

1. On your **Dashboard** page, click on **Campaigns** in the left sidebar and in the **Campaigns** panel that opens, click **Create Campaign**.
2. In the **Campaign Builder** page, in the **Details** tab at the top, enter a campaign name and select your brand from the dropdown.
3. In the **Leads** section, assign leads by selecting from an existing list or uploading a CSV directly.
4. In the **Template** section, choose an existing email template or click **Create New Template** to write one on the spot.
5. In the **Sequence** section, configure your follow-up sequence with delays and conditions.
6. In the **Settings** section, set your send window (time of day) and enable A/B test variants if desired.
7. Review everything on the **Summary** page, then click **Start Campaign** to begin sending.

That's it! Now your campaign is live and emails will start sending according to your schedule.

## Notes

- Campaigns can use multiple brands for rotation to spread volume across inboxes.
- Leads can be filtered by status before assignment to avoid duplicates or unqualified contacts.
- Sequences automatically stop if a lead replies or unsubscribes, protecting your sender reputation.
- Send windows respect the lead's timezone when that data is available.
- **Start** means active sending. **Pause** stops new sends but keeps scheduled emails in queue. **Cancel** permanently stops everything.

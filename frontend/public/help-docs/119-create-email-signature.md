# How to Create an Email Signature for Your Brand

**Last Update:** July 2026

**Post ID:** 119

A professional email signature is one of the fastest ways to build trust with a cold prospect. It tells the recipient who you are, what company you represent, and how to reach you. If you are running a multi brand cold outreach operation, every brand needs its own signature to stay consistent and credible. Learning how to create email signature coldaf configurations ensures that every campaign email closes with the right identity, without you having to paste it manually each time.

A well-designed brand email signature cold outreach element also reinforces brand recognition. When recipients see the same logo, name, and contact details across multiple touchpoints, they are more likely to remember you. Setting up a proper cold email signature setup in ColdAF takes only a minute and saves hours of repetitive work across campaigns.

## You can create an email signature in just a few steps. Here's how to do it:

1. On your **Dashboard** page, click on **Brands** in the left sidebar and in the panel that opens, select the brand you want to add a signature to.
2. In the **Brands** detail view, click the **Email Signatures** tab at the top of the page.
3. In the **Email Signatures** panel, click **Create Signature** at the top-right.
4. In the **Signature Name** field, enter an internal label like "Default" or "Sales Team" so you can distinguish it later.
5. In the **Name** field, enter the sender's full name. In the **Title** field, enter their job title. In the **Company** field, enter the brand or company name.
6. In the **Phone** and **Website** fields, add the relevant contact details. These will be clickable in most email clients.
7. Click the **Logo / Image** upload area and select a brand logo or headshot image from your computer. Keep it under 200 KB for fast loading.
8. In the **Body Text** editor at the bottom, write any additional lines you want to include, such as a short tagline, social link, or legal disclaimer.
9. Review the preview on the right side, then click **Save Signature** at the bottom of the form.
10. If you want this signature to be used automatically, click **Set as Default** in the signature list.

That's it! Now your signature will be automatically injected into every campaign email sent under this brand.

## Notes

- Each brand can have multiple signatures. This is useful if different team members send on behalf of the same brand.
- One signature per brand can be set as default. The default signature is auto-injected into campaign emails unless you manually choose another.
- Keep the signature under 5 lines for mobile readability. Long signatures look cluttered on phones and can trigger spam filters.
- Include the unsubscribe link in the campaign footer separately, not inside the signature block. ColdAF handles unsubscribe management at the campaign level.
- Signature images are hosted and served via CDN. Use a clear, retina-friendly image for best results.

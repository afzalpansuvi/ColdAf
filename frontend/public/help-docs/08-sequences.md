# How to Create Follow-Up Sequences in ColdAF

**Last Update:** July 2026

**Post ID:** 108

Most cold email replies do not come from the first message. They come from the third, fourth, or fifth touch—when the timing finally lines up and the prospect is ready to talk. That is why a strong **cold email follow up sequence** is one of the highest-leverage parts of any outreach strategy. With ColdAF, you can build multi-step sequences that keep your brand in front of leads without becoming annoying, and automate the process so no opportunity falls through the cracks.

If you are looking for **automated follow up emails** that stop automatically when a lead replies or unsubscribes, ColdAF has you covered. The **Sequence Builder** gives you full control over timing, copy, and exit conditions, so your outreach feels personal at every step. Learning how to build **cold outreach sequences** inside ColdAF means you create the system once, then let it run across every campaign you launch.

## You can create follow-up sequences in just a few steps. Here's how to do it:

1. Start with your main navigation, click on **Sequence Builder** in the left sidebar at the center and in the page that opens, click **Create Sequence**.

2. In the **Sequence Builder**, in the **Name** field at the top, enter a name for your sequence (for example, "SaaS Founder 3-Step Follow-Up"). Then click **Add Step** to create the first follow-up email.

3. In the step editor, in the **Delay** dropdown at the top, set the timing for this step (for example, **3 days after initial email**). This means the follow-up will be sent three days after the first campaign email is delivered.

4. In the **Body** editor at the center, write the follow-up email message. You can also click **AI Generate** to have ColdAF write the step for you based on the tone of the original campaign.

5. Click **Add Step** again to create a second follow-up. In the **Delay** dropdown, set the timing (for example, **7 days after step 1**). Write or generate the email body for this step as well.

6. In the **Stop Conditions** panel at the bottom, enable the conditions you want. ColdAF can automatically stop the sequence if the lead **replies**, **unsubscribes**, or **bounces**. This prevents over-messaging and protects your sender reputation.

7. Click **Save Sequence** at the top right. Your sequence is now ready to use across campaigns.

8. Go to your **Campaigns** page, select the campaign you want to assign the sequence to, and in the campaign settings, select your saved sequence from the **Follow-Up Sequence** dropdown. Click **Save**.

That's it! Now your campaign will automatically send follow-ups on schedule and stop when a lead engages.

## Notes

- Sequences are **reusable** across multiple campaigns. Build one sequence and assign it to as many campaigns as you want without recreating it each time.
- Each step can use a **different template**, so your follow-ups can shift tone or angle as the sequence progresses.
- **Stop conditions** prevent over-messaging. If a lead replies, unsubscribes, or bounces, the remaining steps will not send.
- **AI-generated follow-ups** adapt tone based on the original campaign template, so the sequence feels consistent rather than robotic.
- Sequences respect the campaign's **send window settings**, so follow-ups only go out during your configured delivery hours.

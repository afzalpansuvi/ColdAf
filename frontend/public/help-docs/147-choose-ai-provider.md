# How to Choose Your AI Provider in ColdAF

**Last Update:** July 2026

**Post ID:** 147

Not all AI models write cold emails the same way. If you are trying to choose ai provider coldaf options that match your brand voice and budget, understanding the differences between Claude, GPT, and Gemini is essential. The right ai model selection cold outreach strategy can mean the difference between generic copy and emails that actually get replies. When you compare claude vs gpt cold email output, you will notice that Claude leans toward analytical and structured prose, while GPT tends to be more creative and conversational. Gemini offers a fast, cost-effective alternative that works well for simpler sequences. Picking the right provider ensures your templates sound consistent with your brand and your audience.

ColdAF lets you configure a default provider at the organization level while still allowing you to override it per template if needed. This flexibility is important because different campaigns may benefit from different writing styles. A technical B2B campaign might prefer Claude's precise tone, while a playful consumer brand might want GPT's creative flair. Setting up the provider correctly from the start saves you from regenerating templates later and keeps your costs predictable.

## You can choose your AI provider in just a few steps. Here's how to do it:

1. Go to **Settings** from the main navigation.
2. Click **AI Settings** in the settings menu.
3. Review the available providers: **Claude** (Anthropic), **GPT** (OpenAI), and **Gemini** (Google).
4. Compare the model options listed for each provider. Claude tends to produce analytical, long-form copy. GPT is generally more creative and versatile. Gemini is fast and cost-effective for high-volume generation.
5. Select your default provider by clicking the radio button next to your preferred option.
6. Choose a model tier within that provider, such as Claude Haiku for speed or Claude Sonnet for deeper reasoning.
7. Enter your API key for the chosen provider in the secure input field.
8. Click **Save** to store your configuration.
9. Test the setup by clicking the sample generation button to confirm the connection works and the output meets your expectations.

That's it! Now you have a default AI provider configured for all your generation tasks.

## Notes

- You can use different providers for different brands if you need distinct tones across business units.
- Claude excels at business context, formal tone, and structured reasoning for complex value propositions.
- GPT is best for creative copy, catchy subject lines, and conversational outreach.
- Gemini is fastest and cheapest, making it ideal for high-volume warmup or simple sequences.
- API keys are stored encrypted and never exposed in the frontend.
- All providers support the same variable injection, so switching does not break your templates.
- You can switch providers anytime without losing previously saved templates.
- If your API key is invalid or rate-limited, ColdAF will show an error in the generation panel.

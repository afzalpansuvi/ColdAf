# How to Export Leads to a CSV File in ColdAF

**Last Update:** July 2026

**Post ID:** 141

Sometimes you need to take your lead data outside ColdAF. Whether you are creating a backup, importing into a CRM, or running an external analysis, knowing how to **export leads csv coldaf** is a critical skill. This guide explains the **download leads cold email** process so you can extract your contact list in a standard format. Learning how to **export cold outreach contacts** gives you full control over your data and makes it easy to share with team members or external tools. Regular exports are also a good practice for data redundancy and reporting workflows that live outside ColdAF. You can export everything or just a filtered slice depending on your current needs.

## You can export leads to a CSV file in just a few steps. Here's how to do it:

1. Go to **Leads** from the main navigation.
2. Apply filters if you only want a subset of leads, for example, by status, source, or campaign.
3. Click **Export** near the top of the leads list.
4. Select **CSV** as the export format.
5. Select which fields you want to include in the export file.
6. Click **Download** to generate the file.
7. The file will download to your computer. Open it in Excel, Google Sheets, or any spreadsheet tool.

That's it! Now you can **export leads csv coldaf** and use your data wherever you need it.

## Notes

- The export includes all visible leads or the filtered subset you have selected.
- Common use cases include exporting for CRM import, creating backups, or performing external data analysis.
- The CSV includes all standard lead fields such as name, email, company, title, industry, and status.
- Export respects whatever filters are currently active, so you can export just the leads you need.
- Large lists may take a moment to generate. Wait for the download prompt before closing the page.
- The exported file is automatically named with a timestamp so you can track versions easily.
- You can also export leads in filtered views to create targeted segments for external use or team handoffs.
- Exported files include all selected fields in the order shown in the export dialog, so you can customize the layout for your destination system.

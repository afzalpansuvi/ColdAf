## You can track AI token usage and costs in just a few steps. Here's how to do it:

1. Go to **AI Usage** from the main navigation menu.
2. Review the **Total Tokens** card, which shows the combined input and output tokens consumed across all generation activity.
3. Check the **Estimated Cost** card for a running total of spend in your preferred currency.
4. View the **Usage Over Time** chart to see a daily breakdown of token consumption and associated costs.
5. Scroll to the cost breakdown table to see usage split by provider and model, such as Claude Haiku versus GPT-4o.
6. Review the **AI Usage Limits** progress bars to see how close you are to your monthly caps.
7. Check if you are nearing any limits, and if so, note which model is driving the most consumption.
8. Adjust your limits in **Settings** under **AI Settings** if you need to raise or lower your monthly thresholds.

That's it! Now you can monitor your AI spend and avoid unexpected overages.

## Notes

- Input tokens include your prompt plus any lead context and brand instructions passed to the model.
- Output tokens are the AI-generated email text returned by the provider.
- Costs vary by model: Claude Haiku is typically the cheapest, GPT-4o is moderate, and Claude Sonnet is higher.
- Set monthly limits to control spend and prevent runaway generation during testing.
- Usage resets on the first of each month based on your organization billing cycle.
- Export usage data to CSV for accounting or client reporting.
- Team usage is aggregated per organization, so you see all members in one view.
- If you hit a limit, generations will pause until the cap is raised or the reset date passes.

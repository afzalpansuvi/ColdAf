# How to Set a Custom AI Prompt for Your Brand

**Last Update:** July 2026

**Post ID:** 118

AI-generated emails only work when they sound like a real person wrote them. If you want every message to feel on-brand, you need to define the voice, tone, and style that the AI should follow. Setting a brand ai prompt coldaf ensures that Claude, GPT, and Gemini all write in the same consistent voice for a specific client or business unit. This is especially important when you are scaling multi brand cold outreach and cannot personally draft every single email.

A well-crafted cold email ai tone setting transforms generic AI output into persuasive, industry-specific copy. Whether you want a "professional but friendly SaaS founder persona" or a "direct B2B sales executive" style, the prompt you write becomes the invisible editor for every AI-generated campaign. Learning how to customize ai brand voice cold outreach is one of the highest-leverage skills you can build in ColdAF.

## You can set a custom AI prompt in just a few steps. Here's how to do it:

1. On your **Dashboard** page, click on **Brands** in the left sidebar and in the panel that opens, select the brand you want to configure.
2. In the **Brands** detail view, click the **AI Prompt** tab at the top of the page.
3. In the **Brand Description** text area at the top of the form, write a short paragraph about what the company does, who it serves, and what makes it unique.
4. In the **Tone** field below it, describe the desired voice in plain language. For example, "Professional but friendly SaaS founder persona" or "Warm, consultative, and slightly informal."
5. In the **Example Emails** section, paste two or three real emails that reflect the ideal style. The AI will use these as reference patterns.
6. In the **What to Avoid** text area, list phrases, tones, or approaches that should never appear. For example, "avoid buzzwords," "no aggressive sales language," or "do not use emojis."
7. In the **Tone Keywords** field, enter comma-separated descriptors like "helpful, concise, confident, data-driven."
8. Review the full prompt, then click **Save Prompt** at the bottom of the form.

That's it! Now every AI-generated email for this brand will follow the voice, tone, and constraints you defined.

## Notes

- The AI prompt guides all AI-generated emails for this brand. This includes one-off generations, sequence steps, and follow-up drafts created inside the **AI Chat** or **AI Agent** modules.
- Be specific about tone, industry, and target audience. Vague prompts like "write good emails" produce vague results. The more detail you give, the better the output.
- Include example intros and CTAs in the **Example Emails** section. These are the most important parts of the message for the AI to mimic.
- The prompt is sent alongside your request to Claude, GPT, and Gemini depending on your **AI Usage** settings. Each model will interpret the prompt, so clarity matters more than clever formatting.
